WEIGHTLIFTING
I started lifting for high school sports. I became more serious in my junior year at Penn with the help of several lifting buddies and good YouTubers. I wanted to compete in Mr./Mrs. Penn in Spring 2020 before COVID-19, but I'll keep training at home for now to compete someday.
GUITAR
I started in middle school to emulate my older brother and loved to play hard rock, punk rock, and metal - Led Zepplin, Extreme, Metallica, Sum 41, Weezer. Now, I've been learning riffs from Vulfpeck, Shakey Graves, Theo Katzman, and Allen Stone.

I've been playing on/off for 10 years and often switch genres, but I always know how to play 1) Stop this Train\\John Mayer 2) Blackbird\\Beatles 3) Wind Cries Mary\\Jimi Hendrix 4) Sweet Child O' Mine\\Guns N'Roses 5) Bewitched\\Ella Fitzgerald

COACHING
I've coached, cheered on, played in, and organized Special for the past 9 years. If you're able to volunteer, do it! I started Penn Special Olympics . We work closely with Special Olympics Philadelphia and Pennsylvania to host an annual statewide flag football event , lead a Young Athletes program at Christian Street YMCA, fundraise via the Polar Plunge , and bring in cool speakers to Penn's campus.
